Biography

I’m a first year Master student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of California San Diego (UCSD), focusing on Signal and Image Processing and Machine Learning. I am also a research assistant advised by Prof. Xinyu Zhang.

I earned my bachelor's degree in communication engineering with honors from Tianjin University in 4 years in 2022. I was a research assistant advised by Professor Yuan Zhou during my undergraduate study. After graduation, I worked as a full-time 4G/5G Integration & Verification Engineer in ERICSSON.
